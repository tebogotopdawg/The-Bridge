About ACTivate 2025

ACTivate Film and Theatre Conference is a national platform dedicated to reawakening the spirit of storytelling and cultural expression in Mpumalanga through the powerful mediums of film and theatre.

🎬

Innovation Focus

Reimagining storytelling through technology, inclusivity, and sustainability to build future-ready creative industries.

🤝

Networking

Networking at ACTivate connects dreamers, creators, and leaders to spark collaborations that transform storytelling futures.

🎭

Hands-on Learning

Workshops at ACTivate provide hands-on learning, empowering participants with practical skills for creative industries.

Featured Speakers

Learn from industry pioneers and thought leaders

🎭

Dr. Jerry Mofokeng wa Makhetha

Acclaimed Actor & Storyteller

South African stage and screen actor who has appeared in critically acclaimed films including Cry The Beloved Country, Lord of War, and the Academy Award-winning film Tsotsi.

📺

Jack Devnarain

Television & Film Actor

South African actor best known for his roles in television serials The Ghost and the Darkness, Isidingo, and Mayfair.

🎨

Mmabatho Montsho

Multi-disciplinary Artist & Director

South African fine artist, writer, actress, and film director known for her versatile creative expression across multiple mediums.
